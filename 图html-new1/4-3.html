策略 1（均匀衰减）：\(p_l = p_1 \cdot r^{l-1}\)，\(r\in(0,1)\)，公比 \(r\approx\mu_\star(P)\)
策略 2（谱间隙驱动）：\(p_l = \max\{p_{\min},\;p_{\text{base}}\exp(-\kappa\lambda_{\text{gap}}(l{-}1))\}\)
配额公式：\(k_i^{(l)} = \bigl\lfloor k_i^{\text{total}} \cdot \rho_l \,/\, \textstyle\sum_{l'}\rho_{l'}\bigr\rfloor\)，\(\rho_l:=p_l^{-\gamma}\)，\(\gamma\in[1/2,1]\)
约束：\(\sum_{l=1}^{L} k_i^{(l)} = k_i^{\text{total}}\)；\(p_1 \geq p_2 \geq \cdots \geq p_L\)；\(p_l\geq p_{\min}=0.1\)
